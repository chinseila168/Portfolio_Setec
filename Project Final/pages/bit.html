About MIS

Definition: Business Information Technology, often shortened to BIT, refers to the use of modern information technology in the business context. It involves the application of technology to improve business processes, solve business problems, and achieve business objectives. Focus Areas: Business Information Technology encompasses a range of areas such as enterprise resource planning (ERP), customer relationship management (CRM), business intelligence, e-commerce, and more. Role: Professionals in BIT play a crucial role in aligning technology solutions with business goals, ensuring efficient use of IT resources, and facilitating communication between IT and business stakeholders.

Accounting

Description: Accounting is the systematic recording, analysis, and reporting of financial transactions of a business. It involves maintaining financial records, preparing financial statements, and ensuring compliance with relevant regulations. Skills and Knowledge Areas: Financial reporting, auditing, taxation, budgeting, financial analysis, and understanding of accounting principles.

Marketing

Description: Marketing involves creating, communicating, delivering, and exchanging offerings that have value for customers. It includes various activities such as market research, advertising, branding, and sales. Skills and Knowledge Areas: Market research, digital marketing, branding, advertising, consumer behavior, communication, and strategic planning.

Graphic Design

Focus: Visual communication through the use of images, typography, and layout. Skills: Adobe Creative Suite (e.g., Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), layout design, color theory, branding, and user experience design. Career Paths: Graphic Designer, Art Director, UX/UI Designer, Branding Specialist.

Sales Management

Description: Sales management involves planning, directing, and controlling the sales activities of a business. It includes setting sales targets, developing sales strategies, managing sales teams, and building customer relationships. Skills and Knowledge Areas: Sales strategy, customer relationship management (CRM), negotiation, team leadership, and understanding of market trends.

Programming and Database

Focus: Designing and developing software applications. Skills: Programming languages (e.g., Java, Python, C#), software architecture, algorithms, debugging, and collaboration. Career Paths: Software Developer, Software Engineer, Full-stack Developer, DevOps Engineer.

Web Development

Description: Programming involves writing code to develop software applications, while database management involves designing, implementing, and managing databases to store and retrieve data efficiently. Skills and Knowledge Areas: Programming languages (e.g., Java, Python, C#), software development methodologies, database design, SQL, and problem-solving.

Studio Multimaedia

Description: Studio multimedia refers to the creation and production of multimedia content within a studio environment. This field involves integrating various forms of media, such as graphics, audio, video, and interactive elements, to create engaging and dynamic content.

Each major offers unique challenges and opportunities. The choice often depends on individual interests, career goals, and the specific aspects of technology and creativity that resonate with the individual

ABOUT US

Vision

SETEC continues to become a leading private establishment in providing tertiary education in Information Technology and Design in Cambodia and promote quality in education, management, researches and services to an international standard.

Mission

In order to achieve its vision, SETEC educates and trains students to attain quality of knowledge and ethical values. This will assist them in grasping the job opportunities. To this end SETEC focuses on staff and teacher development, student services, curriculum modernization and facility improvement.

Goal

SETEC is committed to achieve 5 main goals.
1/ To promote quality of curriculum development so as to respond to Cambodian educational policy as well as national and international job market.
2/ To continue to enhance quality of tertiary education.
3/ To promote infrastructure and facility.
4/ To promote staff and teacher development.
5/ To strengthen student services to ensure that students achieve success in their study and innovation.

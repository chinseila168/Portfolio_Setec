Architecture DESIGN

Description: Architecture design involves planning, designing, and overseeing the construction of buildings and structures. Architects consider functionality, aesthetics, safety, and the environmental impact of their designs. Key Aspects: Spatial planning, building materials, structural systems, sustainable design, aesthetics, and user experience.

Interior Design

Description: Interior design focuses on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing interior spaces. Interior designers consider furniture, color schemes, lighting, and spatial arrangements to enhance the overall atmosphere and usability of a space. Key Aspects: Space planning, furniture selection, color coordination, lighting design, and material selection.

Landscape Design

Description: Landscape design involves planning and designing outdoor spaces, including gardens, parks, and outdoor areas around buildings. Landscape designers consider natural elements, climate, and user needs to create harmonious and sustainable outdoor environments. Key Aspects: Plant selection, hardscape design, environmental sustainability, terrain considerations, and user interaction.

Studio PHOTOGRAPHY

Description: Studio photography is a form of photography where images are captured within a controlled environment, typically a photography studio. Photographers use various lighting setups, backdrops, and props to achieve desired effects. Key Aspects: Lighting techniques, composition, use of backdrops, equipment (cameras, lenses, lighting), and post-processing.

Exterior Design

Description: Exterior design focuses on the aesthetics and functionality of the outer appearance of buildings and outdoor spaces. It includes the design of facades, landscaping, and outdoor elements to create a visually appealing and cohesive exterior. Key Aspects: Facade design, outdoor materials, landscaping, outdoor furniture, and environmental considerations.

Landscape Design

Description: Multimedia design involves creating content that incorporates a combination of various media elements such as text, graphics, audio, video, and animations. It is commonly used in web design, advertising, and interactive presentations. Key Aspects: Graphic design, animation, audiovisual elements, interactive design, and user engagement.

Studio Multimaedia

Description: Studio multimedia refers to the creation and production of multimedia content within a studio environment. This field involves integrating various forms of media, such as graphics, audio, video, and interactive elements, to create engaging and dynamic content.

Each of these design fields requires a unique skill set, creativity, and a deep understanding of the principles and elements associated with that specific area of design. Professionals in these fields often collaborate to create comprehensive and cohesive designs for a variety of projects.

ABOUT US

Vision

SETEC continues to become a leading private establishment in providing tertiary education in Information Technology and Design in Cambodia and promote quality in education, management, researches and services to an international standard.

Mission

In order to achieve its vision, SETEC educates and trains students to attain quality of knowledge and ethical values. This will assist them in grasping the job opportunities. To this end SETEC focuses on staff and teacher development, student services, curriculum modernization and facility improvement.

Goal

SETEC is committed to achieve 5 main goals.
1/ To promote quality of curriculum development so as to respond to Cambodian educational policy as well as national and international job market.
2/ To continue to enhance quality of tertiary education.
3/ To promote infrastructure and facility.
4/ To promote staff and teacher development.
5/ To strengthen student services to ensure that students achieve success in their study and innovation.
